Pancakes

List of Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Egg

If you are a little kid, be sure to have a guardian with you

Steps to Follow:

  1. Pour a flour
  2. Pour 2 cups of milk
  3. break the egg
  4. Mix together
  5. Make the pan heated and put little oil
  6. Pour the liquid
  7. Wait until it is done
  8. flip to the other side
  9. when the other side is done, put it into a plate and ENJOY!
